Device Description

The Xiaomi 12S ultra. The ultimate flagship and camera phone.

The smartphone pushes the boundaries when it comes to what a smartphone camera could do. The device features an 6.7 inch LTPO 1440p display with 120Hz. The display also supports 10 bit colors. There is the Snapdragon 8+ gen processor which is made using more power saving process than the Snapdragon 8 Gen ensuring higher clock speeds on the GPU and CPU cores. The main camera has a 1 inch sensor with 50MP and also has two more 48 MP cameras. There is 5x telephoto lens. You can record 4k videos and that too in best quality. On top of all that there is IP67 waterproof rating for the device. The battery lasts a whole day and it also has fast charging so that you could be back again. The cpu benchmarks are one of the best in the world.
