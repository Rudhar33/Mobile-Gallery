The Iphone 11. It was the most sold iphone when it was launched back in 2019. Still the demand for this device is at par with others. This is due to the value it provides for its price. We would say this would be the best choice if you are thinking to switch to Iphone but dont want to break the bank.

It features dual 12MP camreas which have wide and ultra wide capabilities. There is also a 12MP front camera. The display is LCD usit with best in class usability due to its size. It is available in 6 colors which are mostly pop colors. There is also 4K 60fps video recording in all the cameras. The smartphone is 4G capable meaning it can access internet faster. With all these features there is also IOS 16 which is the latest OS from Apple and ready to be installed on the Iphone 11. These could be paired with the Apple watch or the airpods to further enhance the experience. You can opt for Apple care + as an monthly plan to keep you device secured.
